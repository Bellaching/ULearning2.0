ABOUT US

About

We designed this app for children who lack basic math knowledge. It serves as an e-learning platform to help learners enhance their mathematical skills. U-Learn can provide access to education and training to people who may not have access to traditional classroom-based education, such as those in remote or underserved areas. U-learn platforms can be more cost-effective than traditional classroom-based learning, as they require fewer resources and can reach a larger students.

History

This app started when we were given options in various categories, like education, government, and health centers. We decided to focus on education and create an app to help children understand mathematics. Our aim was to develop an engaging platform that presents mathematical concepts through interactive activities and lessons. We wanted to make learning math enjoyable and accessible to young learners, providing them with a user-friendly and intuitive learning experience.
